My Background and Experience


I’ve had an interest in both the arts and technology as long as I can remember. I began my academic studies with English (more specifically - Literature, Writing and Film) at Arizona State University. While I still enjoy English and the arts, and I always will, I felt that I would like to pursue my interests in technology in regards to a career path.

So, I decided to go back to school. I discovered I really enjoyed working on hardware and building computers, taking things apart and putting them back together. I also discovered that I really enjoy coding – I love being able to create something new by giving instructions to a computer. While an IT career is certainly a more technical endeavor, it still allows for me to think and act in creative ways.

In 2016 I finished an Associate’s Degree at Greenville Technical College. Now, I’m eager to begin a career in the IT industry.

As a recent graduate, I only have a couple years of experience with the skills listed above. However, I'm eager to learn and I have spent time learning more about each of them. More recently, I’ve felt like it would be worthwhile to try to focus more and really try to take my skills in these different programing languages up to the next level. I’ve been focusing on studying different things at different times in an effort keep myself sharp, while also learning new things each time I go to study. For information on what I’m currently working on, thinking about or otherwise focusing on, check out the blog. This page will be updated from time to time as my current focus shifts.
