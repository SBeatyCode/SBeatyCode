Welcome to my blog! A quick introduction.


- Stephen Beaty - 4/7/17

Recently I've been doing a lot of studying and practicing with Web Development. That was the initial inspiration as to why I decided to redesign my website. I wanted something that looked better, cleaner, more modern, and something that showed a bit more of my personality with the design. I also didn’t want to have to rely on Bootstrap. While I feel that Bootstrap and similar frameworks and libraries are important, I felt that it was important that I coded this website by hand, from scratch. I want to keep adding features to this site. I may tweak the design a bit, depending on how I think it looks within a few months’ time. I also want to add in full database/PHP integration with the Blog. Currently, articles are all hardcoded directly. I feel that having the articles and details about the articles pulled from a database for virtually all the blog pages (except for the individual full article pages perhaps) would be a lot more efficient. I'd also like to potentially make my own personal "companion app" to make writing new blog posts for myself easier. For now, I'm happy with everything I've accomplished, and excited to see how it will grow.
